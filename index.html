About Me

My name is Megan Lockette. I'm 26 years old and I'm from Oakland, California. Growing up, I wanted to be every profession you could possibly think of, from a school teacher to a Victoria Secret Angel, just to walk down the runway in huge and extravgant angel wings. In 2011, straight out of my senior year of high school, I went to San Jose State University and majored in Business Management. I had my heart set on being a fashion buyer. Traveling and shopping sounded like a dream job to me! Well into my third year, I failed my first class ever!!! It was calculus for "business and avaiation students". I remember thinking, "Why am I even in the same math class as aviation students?" I thought long and hard and began reflecting back on the courses I took outside of my major that I really enjoyed. The one that stood out the most was Sociology 101. I knew then I had to drop my current major and take the necessary steps to switch to Sociology.

My mom has always been a role model to me, so when she suggested for the thousandth time, "Why don't you learn how to code?", I listened. At this point in my life, I was realizing just how much my current career was taking a toll on my mental health and everyday mood. Spending majority of my days with previously homeless families and individuals, counseling them about how to deal with their past traumas and unresolved issues was becoming overwhelming. I wanted a job that I could enjoy coming to and when I learned exactly what coding was, I was all in!
